Trézor.io/start | Starting Up Your Device | Trezor®

Setting up your Trezor® device is a crucial first step to secure your cryptocurrency holdings. This guide walks you through the simple process of starting up your Trezor device at Trézor.io/start, so you can confidently manage your digital assets.

Getting Started with Trezor


  • 1. Go to Trézor.io/start

    Visit Trézor.io/start to begin the setup process. Here, you’ll find resources and guides for various Trezor models, including Trezor One and Trezor Model T.

  • 2. Install Trezor Bridge

    For smooth communication between your Trezor device and computer, download and install Trezor Bridge. This software enables secure access to your device and is essential for the initial setup.

  • 3. Follow On-Screen Setup Instructions

    Once Trezor Bridge is installed, connect your Trezor device to your computer and follow the on-screen instructions. You’ll be prompted to create a PIN and record your recovery seed—a critical step to ensure you can recover your assets if needed.

Why Choose Trezor for Crypto Security?

Trezor is a leading hardware wallet that offers high-level security to protect your crypto assets from cyber threats. With Trezor, you gain peace of mind knowing your cryptocurrencies are stored offline and away from hackers.

Securing Your Recovery Seed

The recovery seed is a series of words unique to your wallet. Write it down on the provided card and store it in a secure location. Never share it online or with anyone else, as it’s the key to your funds.


Conclusion

Starting up your Trezor device at Trézor.io/start is a straightforward process that reinforces your digital asset security. With these steps, you can confidently safeguard your cryptocurrency investments.
